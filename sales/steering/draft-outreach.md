# Draft Outreach

Research first, then draft. Never sends generic outreach — always researches the prospect first to personalize the message.

## Triggers

"draft outreach to [person/company]", "write cold email to [prospect]", "reach out to [name]"

## How It Works

1. **Research** (always first): Web search + enrichment (if connected) + CRM (if connected)
2. **Draft**: Personalized opening, relevant hook, clear CTA
3. **Deliver**: Email draft (if connected), LinkedIn copy (always), output to user (always)

## Output Format

```markdown
# Outreach Draft: [Person] @ [Company]

## Research Summary
**Target:** [Name], [Title] at [Company]
**Hook:** [Why reaching out now]
**Goal:** [What you want from this outreach]

## Email Draft
**To:** [email if known]
**Subject:** [Personalized subject line]

[Email body — plain text, no markdown formatting]

**Subject Line Alternatives:**
1. [Option 2]
2. [Option 3]

## LinkedIn Message (if no email)
**Connection Request (< 300 chars):**
[Short, no-pitch connection request]

**Follow-up Message:**
[Value-first message]

## Why This Approach
| Element | Based On |
|---------|----------|
| Opening | [Research finding] |
| Hook | [Their priority/pain point] |
| Proof | [Relevant customer story] |
| CTA | [Low-friction ask] |

## Follow-up Sequence (Optional)
**Day 3:** [Short, new angle]
**Day 7:** [Different value prop]
**Day 14:** [Break-up / final attempt]
```

## Hook Priority Order

1. Trigger event (funding, hiring, news) — most timely
2. Mutual connection — social proof
3. Their content (post, article, talk) — shows research
4. Company initiative — relevant to priorities
5. Role-based pain point — least personal but still relevant

## Email Structure (AIDA)

```
SUBJECT: [Personalized, <50 chars, no spam words]

[Opening: Personal hook - shows you researched them]

[Interest: Their problem/opportunity in 1-2 sentences]

[Desire: Brief proof point - similar company result]

[Action: Clear, low-friction CTA]
```

## Email Style Guidelines

- Be concise. Get to the point quickly.
- No markdown formatting — no asterisks, bold, or headers. Plain text only.
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max). White space is your friend.
- Simple lists with plain dashes if needed.

## Message Templates

### Cold Outreach
Subject: [Their initiative] + [your angle]. Personal hook → 1 sentence on their challenge → brief proof → CTA.

### Warm Outreach
Reference how you know them → why reaching out now → specific value → CTA.

### Re-Engagement
Acknowledge time passed → new reason to reconnect → simple question to re-open.

### Post-Event Follow-up
Specific memory from conversation → value-add resource → soft CTA.

## What NOT to Do

- Generic openers ("I hope this email finds you well")
- Feature dumps (long paragraphs about your product)
- Fake personalization ("I noticed you work at [Company]")
- Markdown in emails (bold, italic, headers won't render)

## Channel Selection

- Verified email available → email preferred, LinkedIn backup
- No email → LinkedIn connection request + follow-up template
- Warm intro possible → suggest mutual connection outreach first
