# Performance Analytics

Frameworks for measuring, reporting, and optimizing marketing performance across channels and campaigns.

## Key Metrics by Channel

### Email
Delivery rate (95-99%), open rate (15-30%), CTR (2-5%), CTOR (10-20%), unsubscribe (<0.5%), bounce (<2%), conversion (1-5%), list growth (2-5%/mo).

### Social Media
Impressions, reach, engagement rate, CTR, follower growth rate, share/repost rate, video view/completion rate, share of voice.

### Paid Advertising
Impressions, CTR, CPC, CPM, conversion rate, CPA, ROAS, Quality Score, frequency, view-through conversions.

### SEO / Organic
Organic sessions, keyword rankings, organic CTR, pages indexed, domain authority, backlinks, page speed, organic conversion rate.

### Content Marketing
Pageviews, unique visitors, time on page, bounce rate, scroll depth, social shares, backlinks earned, lead generation, content ROI.

### Overall / Pipeline
MQLs, SQLs, MQL→SQL conversion, pipeline generated, pipeline velocity, CAC, CAC payback, marketing-sourced revenue, marketing-influenced revenue.

## Reporting Templates

### Weekly (team standup)
Top 3 metrics with WoW change, what worked, what needs attention, this week's priorities.

### Monthly (stakeholder)
Executive summary → key metrics dashboard → channel performance → campaign highlights → what worked/didn't → recommendations → budget vs plan.

### Quarterly (leadership)
Quarter vs goals → YTD trajectory → channel ROI → campaign summary → competitive observations → strategic recommendations → budget request.

## Trend Analysis

Look for: directional trends (4+ periods), inflection points, seasonality, anomalies, leading indicators.

Process: Chart over time (8-12+ data points) → identify direction → calculate rate of change → overlay events → compare to benchmarks → identify correlations → form hypotheses.

## Attribution Models

| Model | How It Works | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Last touch | 100% to last interaction | Conversion triggers |
| First touch | 100% to first interaction | Top-of-funnel |
| Linear | Equal credit to all | Fair representation |
| Time decay | More credit closer to conversion | Balanced view |
| Position-based | 40/20/40 first/middle/last | Discovery + conversion |
| Data-driven | Algorithmic | Most accurate (needs volume) |

Start with last-touch if no model exists. Use position-based as reasonable middle ground for B2B.

## Optimization Framework

1. Identify underperforming metrics
2. Diagnose funnel stage (impressions → clicks → conversions → retention)
3. Hypothesize cause (audience, message, creative, offer, timing, technical)
4. Prioritize by impact × effort
5. Test with one variable at a time
6. Measure and scale or iterate

### Optimization Cadence
Daily: paid campaign monitoring. Weekly: channel performance review. Bi-weekly: creative refresh. Monthly: full review. Quarterly: strategic channel/budget review.
