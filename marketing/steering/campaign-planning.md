# Campaign Planning

Frameworks for planning, structuring, and executing marketing campaigns.

## Campaign Framework: Objective → Audience → Message → Channel → Measure

### 1. Objective
Awareness, Consideration, Conversion, Retention, or Advocacy. Must be SMART.

### 2. Audience
Demographics, psychographics, behavioral, buying stage. Create a brief profile:
> "[Role] at [company type] struggling with [pain point] looking for [outcome]. Discovers solutions through [channels], cares most about [priorities]."

### 3. Message
- Core message (one sentence)
- 3-4 supporting messages addressing pain points
- Proof points for each
- Differentiation from alternatives

Message hierarchy: Why should I care? → What is the solution? → Why you? → What should I do?

### 4. Channel
Select based on where the audience is, not where you're comfortable. See Channel Selection Guide below.

### 5. Measure
See Success Metrics by Campaign Type below.

## Channel Selection Guide

### Owned
Blog/Website (SEO, thought leadership), Email (nurture, retention), Social organic (awareness, community), Webinars (education, lead gen), Podcast (thought leadership).

### Earned
PR/Media (awareness, credibility), Guest content (audience expansion, SEO), Influencer/Partner, Community, Reviews/Ratings.

### Paid
Search ads (high-intent capture), Social ads (awareness, retargeting, lead gen), Display (awareness, retargeting), Sponsored content, Events/Sponsorships.

Planning process: Start with milestones → work backward → map to funnel stages → batch by theme → balance channels → leave 20% flexibility.

## Budget Allocation

Channel allocation framework: Paid acquisition 30-40%, Content production 20-30%, Events 10-20%, Tools 10-15%, Testing 5-10%.

Principles: Start with highest-confidence channel (60-70% of paid budget), reserve 15-20% for testing, shift monthly based on performance, include 10-15% contingency.

## Success Metrics by Campaign Type

**Awareness**: Reach, brand mentions, share of voice, direct traffic, follower growth.
**Lead Gen**: Total leads, MQLs, CPL, lead-to-MQL rate, pipeline influenced.
**Product Launch**: Signups/trials, activation rate, media coverage, social buzz.
**Retention**: Churn rate change, engagement rate, NPS/CSAT, upsell revenue.
**Event**: Registrations, attendance rate, engagement, post-event conversions.
