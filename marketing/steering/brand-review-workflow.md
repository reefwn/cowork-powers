# Brand Review Workflow

Review marketing content against brand voice, style guidelines, and messaging standards.

## When to Use
When the user wants to review, check, or audit content against brand guidelines.

## Inputs

1. **Content**: pasted, file reference, URL, or multiple pieces for batch review
2. **Brand guidelines**: auto-loaded if configured, otherwise ask or do a general review

## Review Process

### With Brand Guidelines
Evaluate against: voice and tone (match defined attributes, flag deviations), terminology and language (preferred terms, jargon level, product names), messaging pillars (alignment with approved messaging), style guide compliance (grammar, formatting, punctuation per guide).

### Without Brand Guidelines (Generic Review)
Evaluate: clarity (main message clear in first paragraph?), consistency (tone, terms, formatting), professionalism (typos, grammar, appropriate tone).

### Legal/Compliance Flags (Always)
Flag: unsubstantiated claims (superlatives without evidence), missing disclaimers, comparative claims, regulatory language, testimonial issues, copyright concerns.

## Output

### Summary
Overall assessment, biggest strengths (1-2 sentences), most important improvements (1-2 sentences).

### Detailed Findings
| Issue | Location | Severity | Suggestion |
|-------|----------|----------|------------|

Severity: High (contradicts brand, compliance risk), Medium (inconsistent but not damaging), Low (minor style issue).

### Revised Sections
Before/after for top 3-5 highest-severity issues.

### Legal/Compliance Flags
Listed separately with recommended actions.

## Follow Up
Offer to: revise full content, fix just high-severity issues, review additional content, or help document brand voice for future reviews.
