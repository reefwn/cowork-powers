# Close Management

Month-end close checklist, task sequencing and dependencies, status tracking, and common close activities organized by day.

> **Important**: All close activities should be reviewed by qualified financial professionals.

## Month-End Close Checklist

### Pre-Close (Last 2-3 Business Days)
- Send close calendar and deadline reminders
- Confirm cut-off procedures with AP, AR, payroll, treasury
- Verify all sub-systems processing normally
- Complete preliminary bank reconciliation
- Review open POs for potential accruals
- Confirm payroll processing schedule

### Day 1 (T+1)
- Confirm sub-ledger period-end processing complete
- AP accruals, payroll entries, cash receipts/disbursements
- Intercompany transactions, bank reconciliation
- Fixed asset depreciation, prepaid amortization

### Day 2 (T+2)
- Revenue recognition and deferred revenue adjustments
- Remaining accrual JEs
- AR and AP subledger reconciliations
- Inventory adjustments, FX revaluation
- Begin balance sheet reconciliations

### Day 3 (T+3)
- Complete all balance sheet reconciliations
- Adjusting JEs from reconciliation findings
- Intercompany reconciliation and eliminations
- Preliminary trial balance and income statement
- Preliminary flux analysis

### Day 4 (T+4)
- Tax provision entries, equity roll-forward
- Finalize all JEs — soft close
- Draft financial statements (P&L, BS, CF)
- Detailed flux analysis and variance explanations
- Management review

### Day 5 (T+5)
- Final adjustments from management review
- Hard close — lock period in ERP
- Distribute financial reporting package
- Update forecasts, conduct close retrospective

## Task Dependencies

```
Level 1 (No deps): Cash entries, bank statement, payroll, depreciation, prepaid amort, AP accruals, IC posting
Level 2 (→ L1): Bank rec, revenue rec, AR/AP subledger recs, FX reval, remaining accruals
Level 3 (→ L2): All BS recs, IC rec, eliminations, adjusting entries, preliminary TB
Level 4 (→ L3): Tax provision, equity roll-forward, consolidation, draft financials, flux analysis
Level 5 (→ L4): Management review, final adjustments, hard close, reporting package, forecast updates
```

### Critical Path
Cash/AP/AR entries → Subledger recs → BS recs → Tax provision → Draft financials → Management review → Hard close

## Close Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Close duration (business days) | Reduce over time |
| Adjusting entries after soft close | Minimize |
| Late tasks | Zero |
| Reconciliation exceptions | Reduce over time |
| Restatements / corrections | Zero |

## Accelerated 3-Day Close Prerequisites

- Automated recurring JEs
- Continuous reconciliation during the month
- Automated intercompany elimination
- Pre-close activities completed before month-end
- Clear ownership and minimal handoffs
- Real-time sub-system integration
